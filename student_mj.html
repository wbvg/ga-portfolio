MIKE JARRELL

Mike travelled from Canada just for the chance to participate in the WDI course with GA. He has spent nearly a decade in finance working in Europe, where he was most recently trading algorithms for a Hedge Fund. He decided to pursue his passion for travel and entrepreneurship by signing up for this course to help run an ecommerce business he's started in Swtizerland. In addition to this, he hopes to apply the skills he learnt in San Francisco where he is starting a social entrepreneurship program..
